White Water Rafting Company

Our goals is to deliver safe, thrilling, and unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that inspire people to connect with nature and the spirit of adventure. To establish Drigs as the ultimate destination for river rafting, recognized for unmatched outdoor adventures, elite guides, and environmental care.

History

Founded in 2020 by passionate river guides, Drigs was born out of a desire to get people outdoors and onto the water. What started with just a few rafts quickly grew into a trusted whitewater rafting company built on safety, adventure, and respect for nature. Today, we continue to guide thousands of guests down thrilling rapids, creating unforgettable river memories while protecting the waterways we love.
